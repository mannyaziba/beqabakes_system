Planning a bigger celebration?

This pin serves the minority who need something more than a Quick Order — a bigger occasion, a fully custom design, a special cake that takes planning. The reframe from "Custom Order" to "Planning a bigger celebration?" does important work: it positions the slow lane as a planning process, not a product category, and sets lead-time expectations before the conversation starts.

SIA+ Mode Celebration (soft) — warm, aspirational, no urgency
Situation Someone planning a bigger event — a milestone birthday, a wedding, an anniversary. They've seen the quick options and know they need something more. They're looking for confirmation that Beqabakes can do it.
Tension They're worried a fast-gifting bakery won't be able to do what they need, or that they'll be rushed. They need to know this is available and that it's a different, unhurried process.
Solution Custom orders are available. They take more time and planning. Starting the conversation now is the right move. The WA prefill opens a custom order conversation, not the Quick Order flow.
Headline "Planning a bigger celebration?"
Sub-copy "Custom cakes, special designs, personalised flavours. Let's start the conversation."
Lead time Must appear in the post. "48+ hours lead time" or "Best with 2–3 days notice." Sets expectations before WhatsApp — prevents disappointment and saves conversation time.
CTA Let's plan it → link in bio
Bio link → Custom Order path → WA prefill "Hi, I'd like to discuss a custom order"
Visual Lighter, more refined feel than the Quick Order post. Warm neutral tones — cream, blush, natural textures. Show a more elaborate cake if available — tiered, decorated, special occasion feel. Still Cake-Card Wedge: cake in a gift context, not a studio isolation. Four feature points listed (custom design, flavours, packaging, lead time). Brown CTA button.
Tone note Warm and unhurried — the opposite of Express. This buyer is planning, not panicking. No urgency language. No "today" or "fast." The pace signals that this is a considered, quality-focused path.
Pin position Rightmost pin — the specialist path for the considered buyer.
Primary caption Celebration — soft
Situation Planning a bigger celebration? Tension Some occasions deserve something more — a cake that's fully yours, designed for the moment, made with time and care. Solution We do custom orders too. Personalised flavours, special designs, packaging for the occasion. Just give us a little notice — best with 48+ hours. CTA Let's plan it together → link in bio 🎂
