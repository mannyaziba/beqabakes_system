00System foundation

One input. Three outputs. No platform runs its own system.

Each platform kit originally had its own content calendar, its own terminology, its own follow-up logic. That created three parallel systems that contradicted each other. This document replaces all three with a single integrated platform operating system.

The law: No platform creates original content independently. Every post on every platform originates from a COS v1 SIA+ brief. One brief per post slot. Three briefs per week. Each brief produces one feed post per active platform, one Story derivative, and one WhatsApp Status adaptation. Total output: up to 9 pieces from 3 briefs.

Visual rules — all posts

Cake-Card Wedge rule (mandatory)

Every product visual: cake + note card + packaging context. Never cake alone. The wedge is what makes a photo feel like a gift.

Visual hierarchy: cake → context → brand

Cake first (product). Hands/box/note second (emotion). Logo last. Never reverse this order.

Real over styled

Candid delivery moments outperform studio shoots for this brand. Human, contextual, warm — not commercial.

Always use

  • Playfair Display for headlines (product name / situation line)
  • Mulish Medium for price — never smaller than body copy
  • Rosewood (#D16681) for CTA button — only on Express posts
  • Cream Base (#FFE3E9) or Blush (#F7D8DF) as background

Never use

  • Abstract compositions with no product or context
  • Stock photography or generic bakery imagery
  • Over-designed posts with decoration competing with product
05Stories

Daily. Derived from the week's brief. Never standalone.

Stories are the ephemeral layer — they amplify the week's three posts and stay alive for 24 hours. They do not require a new brief. They are adaptations of that day's post brief into a vertical, CTA-forward format.

Story rule: Every Story is a derivative of that week's SIA+ brief. Not a separate piece of content. The brief already defines the situation, mode, and CTA — the Story just reformats it for the vertical, swipe-up context.

Story adaptation by mode

Express

Single product frame. Price displayed. Swipe-up or link sticker to WhatsApp. Tight copy: situation line + CTA only. Rosewood accent.

Celebration

Gift moment photo or delivery video. Warm single-line caption. Link sticker reads "Send this today →". Pink accent.

Retention

Customer photo reshare (with permission) or testimonial screenshot. Soft caption. Link sticker with BACK500 if Friday. Blush palette.

Daily Story minimum — even without a new post

On non-post days, Stories keep the account active in the algorithm and visible to followers. The minimum is one Story. Options in order of preference: reshare a customer delivery photo, share a saved Highlight slide, post an availability status ("We're delivering today — slots available"), or reshare the most recent feed post.

13Content system

Cross-post only. Mon / Wed / Fri. No Sunday post.

Facebook content is derived directly from the same three weekly SIA+ briefs used for Instagram. No original Facebook content is created. The caption may be adapted slightly — Facebook allows and rewards slightly longer copy than Instagram — but the structure, mode, and CTA are identical.

The Sunday post is removed. The original Facebook kit added a Sunday scarcity post with no mode, no brief, and no checklist gate. Adding a fourth content day without system support creates uncontrolled output that drifts from brand voice and blows the weekly time budget.

Cross-post adaptation rules

Keep identical

  • Brand mode assignment
  • CTA text and WA link
  • Image (same post visual)

14Messenger redirect

Messenger captures. WhatsApp closes. Always.

Some Facebook visitors will message via Messenger before finding the WhatsApp link. The Messenger auto-reply acknowledges their message and immediately redirects to WhatsApp. No sales conversation is conducted in Messenger.

FacebookMessenger auto-reply — redirect to WhatsAppTrigger: any Messenger message
Hi! Thanks for reaching out to Beqabakes. We handle all orders on WhatsApp for the fastest service. Tap here to order now → [WA deep link] We'll respond within minutes.
Variables: [WA deep link] · Pre-fills "Hi, I want to send a cake" on open · Do not continue the conversation in Messenger
Why this matters for CRM: If a sales conversation happens in Messenger, it cannot be tagged in the Notion CRM, the Day 7 nudge script cannot fire, and the proof collection trigger is broken. One platform owns conversion. That platform is WhatsApp.

Facebook paid ads — threshold gated

The original Facebook kit suggested "boost top-performing posts" as an optional step with no conditions. This is replaced by the same threshold that governs Instagram ads:

Paid ads activate on Facebook when: (a) Organic content generates 15+ WhatsApp order initiations per week for 4 consecutive weeks, AND (b) at least 10 proof items exist in the bank. Boosting before this threshold is met spends money before organic demand is proven. Not before.
